Our Divisions

Advancing Healthcare Across Multiple Therapeutic Segments

Our specialized divisions are dedicated to addressing diverse healthcare needs through innovative, high-quality pharmaceutical formulations. Each division focuses on delivering evidence-based therapies while maintaining the highest standards of quality, safety, and patient care.

1. General Medicine Division

Status: Active

Comprehensive Healthcare Solutions

A broad portfolio of medicines for the management of common acute and chronic medical conditions, supporting physicians with reliable and effective treatment options.

Focus Areas:
  • Anti-infectives
  • Gastroenterology
  • Pain Management
  • Vitamins & Minerals
  • General Wellness

2. Cardio-Diabetics Division

Status: Active

Heart & Diabetes Care

Dedicated to improving cardiovascular and metabolic health with a comprehensive range of therapies for hypertension, dyslipidemia, diabetes, heart failure, and related disorders.

Focus Areas:
  • Hypertension
  • Diabetes Management
  • Lipid Management
  • Cardiac Care
  • Antiplatelet Therapy

3. Critical Care Division

Status: Active

Life-Saving Therapeutics

Providing advanced injectable medicines and hospital-focused therapies designed for emergency, intensive care, and peri-operative patient management.

Focus Areas:
  • Injectable Antibiotics
  • ICU Medicines
  • Emergency Care
  • Anesthesia Support
  • Hospital Specialties

4. Pediatrics Division

Status: Active

Caring for Every Child

Developing safe, effective, and child-friendly formulations to support healthy growth and treat pediatric illnesses with precision and care.

Focus Areas:
  • Pediatric Antibiotics
  • Nutritional Supplements
  • Pediatric Syrups
  • Respiratory Care
  • Immunity Support

5. Herbals Division

Status: Active

Nature Backed by Science

Offering scientifically developed herbal and nutraceutical products that combine traditional botanical knowledge with modern pharmaceutical quality standards.

Focus Areas:
  • Liver Care
  • Immunity Boosters
  • Women's Health
  • Digestive Health
  • Wellness Products

6. Generic Division

Status: Active

Affordable Healthcare for Everyone

Our Generic Division focuses on providing high-quality, cost-effective pharmaceutical formulations that meet stringent quality standards while improving accessibility to essential medicines. Through partnerships with internationally compliant manufacturing facilities, we deliver reliable generic medicines across multiple therapeutic categories.

Focus Areas:
  • Antibiotics
  • Anti-Diabetic Medicines
  • Cardiovascular Medicines
  • Gastrointestinal Products
  • Analgesics & Anti-Inflammatories
  • Multivitamins & Nutraceuticals
  • Hospital Products
  • Chronic Care Therapies
Key Strengths:
  • Cost-effective healthcare solutions
  • Broad therapeutic portfolio
  • Consistent quality standards
  • Reliable supply chain
  • Institutional & government supply capability
  • Export market opportunities
