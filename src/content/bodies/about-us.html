About Us

About Us

We are constantly searching for innovations that can transform the healthcare landscape. To create a healthier world, we innovate every day. In the beginning, Impact had this vision, and that concept remains even today. Research supports our gut feeling that purpose-driven businesses outperform their competitors, are more inventive, retain and attract the best employees, and understand how to achieve their potential. Impact's mission statement, "From Concept to Commercialization," inspires all we do and shows how committed we are to continuing our tradition as one of the world's greatest contributors. Established in 2004, Impact has expanded into the pharmaceutical, herbal, and skill development training industries, employing over 200+ individuals. Impact Healthcare is a formulation-based pharmaceutical marketing firm that specialises in the ethical marketing of medications for diseases such as NSAIDs, antibiotics, antimalarials, cardio-diabetics, and erectile dysfunction. Strong brands including R Lume Range, Cipotax, AFSC Kit, and others have been produced by the corporation. The company markets brands and goods to more than 15,000 global partners worldwide. One of the company's objectives is to supply high-quality, affordable medications for heart and diabetes conditions.
